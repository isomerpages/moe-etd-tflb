<h2>Our Discovery</h2>
<p>Teachers struggle to manage the demands of monitoring students’ coursework,
from tracking students’ progress to ensuring that they provide quality
feedback to students.
<br>
<br>Many students lack initiative to self-manage their progress and are dependent
on teachers to “chase them”.</p>
<hr>
