<h2>Our Discovery</h2>
<p>Students want to try out practical tasks on their own, but struggle to self-regulate and as a result, continue to be reliant on their teachers for continual guidance.
<br>
<br>Teachers find it difficult to prioritise their attention as students are working individually, progressing at different pace during the lessons.</p>
<hr>
<h2>Our Innovation</h2>
<p><strong>Toolkit for Practical Lessons</strong> includes <strong> a user guide </strong> and some <strong>sample SLS Practical Based Lessons </strong> for the following subjects:</p>

<p>a) Biology</p>
<p>b) Chemistry</p>
<p>c) Food and Nutrition</p>
With the use of these resources, we hope to help teachers support students in a practical lesson and preparing students to be self-regulated learners.

**Assess the guide to find out more about how to utilise the sample templates and structure your practical lesson with SLS**

1.  Biology
2.  Chemistry
3.  Food and Nutrition
